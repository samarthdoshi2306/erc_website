About Us

Hello there! Greetings from Electronics & Robotics Club IIT Bombay! We cater to every individual who ever wanted to design their own circuit and control their own bot.

We are a club that satiates the techie you all possess! The Electronics and Robotics Club was born from the two of its parent clubs(guess which? :p), 2017 being the year of christening. We are headed by our Manager, Jian Vora and our Institute Secretary, Anirudh Mittal. We are a part of ITC, or the Institute Technical Council, IIT Bombay's primary technical body. Other than that we have seven conveners along with many volunteers.

The main function of the club is to organize events like Sessions, Hackathons, General Championships, Competitions, Workshops, etc. throughout the year. We also maintain a tutorial database and a blog and we are ALWAYS open for contribuitions.

We love people contacting us, so drop us a line on Facebook, we'll be waiting!
